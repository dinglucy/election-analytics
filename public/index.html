Introduction to Election Analytics (Week 1)

Introduction This series of blogs leading up to the 2022 US Midterm Elections will aim to predict the outcome for the House of Representatives. This project was created in Gov 1347: Election Analytics, a course taught by Professor Ryan Enos at Harvard University. This first blog aims to explore the two-party vote-share margin for the House of Representatives in order to identify 2014 Midterm Elections First, we examine the 2014 two-party vote-share margin across the United States by state.
